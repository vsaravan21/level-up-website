Build essential skills, one experience at a time

Research shows that 85% of career success depends on interpersonal skills, yet many students and early career professionals struggle to develop them.

level up. turns skill-building into an exciting journey with personalized challenges, growth-tracking tools, and opportunities to compete and connect with friends, helping you unlock your potential.

Features

Build Skills

Build interpersonal skills through bite-sized experiences across four key areas. Our app recommends a unique daily experience, while giving you the freedom to explore at your own pace.

Track Progress

Monitor your growth by earning 'XP' for completed experiences. Our app provides progress graphs that highlight your overall and skill-specific development over time.

Challenge Friends

Challenge your friends to complete experiences, compare XP, and engage with them through the leaderboard. Our app facilitates expanding your circle by adding new friends to join the fun!
